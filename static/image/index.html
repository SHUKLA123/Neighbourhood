About

For over 20 years, The team behind Amipro has focused on making and selling products that inspire people to be more productive, effective and creative when communicating visually. Whether it is ecologically intelligent e3 environmental ceramicsteel™ writing boards, eno interactive boards or a host of other products sourced from across the globe, Whitemark has an unparalleled track record in the visual communication market.

We are the largest producer of ceramicsteel writing boards for the education market in India, supplying a majority of boards that teachers and students use every day. Our products are an integral part of educational environments, businesses, government settings

The core values which AMIPRO has incorporated into its working are honesty, integrity and reliability. With a foundation which is firmly held together by its devotion to ethical values, the company has its eyes firmly fixed towards the future. Constantly evolving its process with time, the products and services of the company have been made innovative and contemporary to match the needs of the modern organizations in a rapidly changing environment.
